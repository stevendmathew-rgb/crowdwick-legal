Child Safety Standards Crowdwick by Aevon Scientific · Last Updated: July 7, 2026 Our Commitment Crowdwick is committed to maintaining a safe environment for all users and has zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) in any form. Crowdwick requires all users to be at least 13 years of age (or 16 in the EEA), enforced through date-of-birth verification at signup. Users who are found to be underage are immediately removed and their data deleted within 72 hours. Prevention Measures Crowdwick employs automated content moderation powered by Google Cloud Vision API for uploaded photos and Google Cloud Video Intelligence API for uploaded videos, screening all user-generated media for explicit content before or immediately after it appears on the platform. Content flagged as violating our policies is automatically removed and the uploader is notified. All user-generated content is subject to review, and users may report any content or account they believe violates our community standards directly within the app. Reporting Mechanisms Users can report any content or account suspected of involving child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or exploitation directly within the Crowdwick app. Reports are reviewed promptly. Any confirmed CSAM is immediately removed, the account is permanently suspended, and the material is reported to the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC) CyberTipline as required by US law (18 U.S.C. § 2258A). Parents or guardians who believe a minor's account exists on Crowdwick may contact us directly at crowdwick@gmail.com with the subject line "CSAM - Minor Account Report." Compliance Crowdwick complies with all applicable child safety laws including COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and reports confirmed CSAM incidents to the appropriate regional and national authorities. We cooperate fully with law enforcement investigations involving child safety. Contact For child safety concerns, reports, or questions about our standards, contact our designated child safety point of contact at crowdwick@gmail.com with "Child Safety" in the subject line. We respond to all child safety reports within 24 hours.
