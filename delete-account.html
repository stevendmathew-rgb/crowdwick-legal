Delete Your Crowdwick Account

Crowdwick by Aevon Scientific  ·  Houston, TX

To permanently delete your Crowdwick account, open the app, go to Profile, scroll to the bottom, and tap Delete Account — no email or support request needed. Once confirmed, your account credentials, username, profile, event decisions, saved events, friend connections, group chats, stories, uploaded media, age verification record, consent records, and event submissions are all permanently removed within 30 days. Uploaded photos and videos may remain in backup storage for up to 90 days before being fully purged, feedback you submitted may be retained in anonymized form for up to 12 months, subscription transaction records may be kept as required by Apple App Store or Google Play billing policies, and certain records may be held longer where required by law. If you can't access the app, email crowdwick@gmail.com with "Account Deletion Request" in the subject line and the email address associated with your account, and we'll process your request within 30 days.
