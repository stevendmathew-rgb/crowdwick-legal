Delete Your Crowdwick Data

Crowdwick by Aevon Scientific  ·  Houston, TX

You can remove specific types of data from within the app at any time without deleting your account — event decisions can be removed from your plan, saved events can be unsaved by tapping the bookmark icon, stories expire automatically after 24 hours or can be deleted manually, group chat messages are removed when you leave or delete a chat, and your profile information (display name, avatar, username) can be updated or cleared at any time from Profile → Edit Profile. If you'd like a copy of your data before deleting anything, go to Profile → Download My Data. To delete everything permanently, you can delete your entire account by going to Profile → Delete Account, which removes all personal data within 30 days (with uploaded media purged from backups within 90 days and anonymized feedback retained for up to 12 months). If you can't access the app or want to request deletion of a specific data type, email crowdwick@gmail.com with "Data Deletion Request" in the subject line, your account email address, and what you'd like deleted, and we'll respond within 30 days.
